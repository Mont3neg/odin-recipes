Easy Cheesy Skillet Lasagna

Description

This recipe is a Lasagna with sausage, mushrooms, and lots of cheese is fast and easy on the stove top- -and there's no compromise on flavor.

It takes 10 minutes to prepare and 35 to cook, and it has 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 Medium onion, diced
  • 1 Pound hot italian sausage
  • 1 Medium red bel pepper, diced
  • 2 Cups sliced mushrooms
  • 1 (24 ounce) jar RAGÚ® Old World Style® Traditional Sauce
  • 1 (9 ounce) package no-boil lasagna sheets, broken into large pieces
  • 1 Cup of water
  • ½ teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
  • 8 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 8 ounces part-skim mozzarella cheese, shredded and divided
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh Italian parsley
